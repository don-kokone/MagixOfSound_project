Confronting your demons has never sounded so beautiful.

On his forthcoming EP, This Is The End, MAGIX of sound delivers an emotionally-rich performance full of fury and hope. MAGIX of sound's music is soulful and dark, and highlighted in vivid colour by the vocal defiance of a survivor.

MAGIX of sound will take you on a cathartic journey, from pop falsettos to vocal crescendos that fill the room and leave you filled with reverence. Experience it live.
